Recommendation System for Books

Dataset

Approach: Collaborate filtering makes book recommendations for a user based on how other users rate books.

Feature engineering: The data is converted as a sparse matrix of ratings, in which each column is a user and each row is a book. Every entry in the matrix is a rating score.

Algorithm: kNN finds the closest book vectors, with cosine similarity as the distance metric.

Analyze and Predict Avocado Prices

Click here to view the full notebook

Dataset: Average price for avocadoes in 52 regions in the US, recorded on a weekly basis from 2015 to 2018. (Kaggle link)

1. Organic vs. Regular Prices
  • Generally, organic and conventional avocado prices have similar fluctuations.
  • There is a sudden drop in organic prices in August 2015.

2. Regional Prices
  • Prices in different regions vary, but follow a similar trend.
  • New York has the highest prices, while Dallas has the lowest.

3. Seasonality and Trend
  • The fluctuations in price is seasonal.
  • The overall trend in price is increasing.

4. Predicted Prices vs. Real Prices

The predicted prices are not exactly the same, but follow the same upward and downward trends of the true prices. The MSE here is $0.01622.

5. Predict Future Prices

The prices predicted by the model capture both the seasonality and the overall increasing trend of the price values.
